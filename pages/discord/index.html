Steps to turn on DarkMode:

Website

1. Log in to discord on the website. Or if you are already logged in, click in the middle right 'Open Discord in your browser'

2. Discord DarkMode should be pre enabled but if not click the settings icon on the bottom left

3. Under 'App settings' click 'Appearance'

4. Then under Theme click 'Dark'

5. Boom you are done!!!
